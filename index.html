Hello world! My name is Enhao Zhang. I'm a senior student majoring in Computer Science at University of Michigan. In the meantime, I'm pursuing another Bachelor’s degree at UM-SJTU Joint Institute, Shanghai Jiao Tong University with Electrical & Computer Engineering major.

My research interests are Database Systems, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and Machine Learning. I have been working with Prof. Nikola Banovic at the Computational HCI Lab. Our research project, Study of Personalized Active Learning, designs a query-based image retrieval system using active learning algorithms, aiming to understand human's role in providing the labelled information and extract maximal information from human through the interaction. I'm also working with Prof. Michael Cafarella in the Video Database Analytics System project, where we are researching and optimizing a video database system which supports binary content-based queries.
